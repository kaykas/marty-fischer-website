About Marty Fischer

One of America's Most Recognized Outdoor Personalities

For nearly four decades, Marty Fischer has been a driving force in the world of sporting clays, combining his passion for the sport with unmatched expertise in course design, instruction, and media.

As the Head Coach of the Georgia Southern Clay Target Team since 2020, Marty has led the team to an impressive four national titles in five years, establishing the program as a powerhouse in collegiate clay target shooting.

Through SportShooting Consultants, Ltd., which he founded in 1993, Marty has designed more than 150 public and private clay target facilities throughout North America. His work has been recognized by Garden & Gun Magazine, which featured six of his designs among the nine best sporting clays courses in the nation, with Foxhall Resort and Sporting Club earning their prestigious "Best of the Best" designation.

A NSCA Level III Certified Instructor with 35+ years of coaching experience, Marty has dedicated his life to sharing his knowledge through multiple channels: as a television host for nearly three decades, author of two acclaimed books, host of the Wing & Clay Nation radio show, and contributor to the nation's leading outdoor publications.

The Knoxville News-Sentinel once referred to him as "perhaps the most sought after gun club designer in America" - a testament to his reputation for excellence and innovation in the field.
