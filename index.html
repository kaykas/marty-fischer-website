Marty Fischer is not just a professional shooter—he is the authority on sporting clays. From championship competition to designing the world's most challenging courses, Marty has shaped the sport for over 35 years.

As Head Coach of Georgia Southern Clay Target Team, he's led the program to 4 national championships in 5 years, establishing them as a collegiate powerhouse.

Through SportShooting Consultants, Ltd., founded in 1993, Marty has designed over 150 facilities across North America. Garden & Gun Magazine featured 6 of his 9 "best sporting clays courses" in their prestigious list.

A NSCA Level III Certified Instructor (the highest level), host of the Wing & Clay Nation podcast, author of two books, and outdoor television personality for nearly three decades on ESPN, Outdoor Channel, and Pursuit Channel.
